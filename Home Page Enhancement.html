Ecuador

Quito, officially San Francisco de Quito, is the capital of the Republic of Ecuador. Likewise, it serves as the capital of the Province of Pichincha and the cantonal head of the Metropolitan District of Quito. It is located in the central north of the inter-Andean region of Ecuador, on the Guayllabamba River basin, on the western slopes of the Pichincha stratovolcano, at an average altitude of 2850 m above sea level. n. m. Quito is considered the highest capital in the world, as long as La Paz is not taken into account.
